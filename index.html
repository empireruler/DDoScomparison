What is DDoS protection?

DDoS(distributed denial of service) protection works by providing a network of servers for customers to scrub out harmful traffic and allow good traffic to be routed without any issues that could prevent a total shut down of the site. With a WAF(Web Application Firewall) customers can filter out harmful traffic like cross site scripting, SQL injections, and credit card theaft.

What are the benefits?

  • no downtime from a large attack - customers can access your site while attacks are cleaned up.
  • keeps data in transit secure -web site providers and customers do not need to worry about credit card and other senstive information being relased.
  • no need for servers on premise - with cloud providers you do not need to worry about housing a global loadbalanced DDoS protection harware in colocation or data centers
